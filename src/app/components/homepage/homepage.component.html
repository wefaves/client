Hi there, I'm Wefaves.
Simple, smart and secure.

Available on :

  • Wefaves experience

    Wefaves is an internet session manager for users who often change the navigation environment. The goal with Wefaves is to allow a user to change media or web browser while retaining its navigation data.

  • Save time & ergonomy

    Providing an ergonomic solution to customers is very important for Wefaves. Wefaves has developed appropriate solutions while maintaining certain ease of use.

    In fact, it will save you time by importing & exporting your navigation environment thanks to Wefaves.

  • Communication

    Communication is important for Wefaves, which is why Wefaves has chosen to set up a blog shortly explaining the different features that are being implemented by developers or just the news of the company.

Simple

By developing a plugin, Wefaves wanted to simplify its solution as much as possible. Thus, with one click, the user can synchronize all his navigation data directly with his profile.

Smart

Wefaves is a solution that wants to be smart. By analyzing the navigation habits of its users, it can propose to them to remove favorites or to give some visibility to some.

Secure

To guarantee the durability of the data, Wefaves directly backs up the data of its users on servers of their own. Thus, Wefaves can vouch for the security of the data of its users.
